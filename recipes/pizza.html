Pizza

Description

A classic Italian dish

Made with fresh ingredients, it's quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 12x9-inch baking pan with foil.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and water. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the dry ingredients.
  4. In a medium mixing bowl, beat together the wet ingredients. Add to dry ingredients one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Add to dry ingredients one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Add remaining ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Remove from oven and cool completely.
