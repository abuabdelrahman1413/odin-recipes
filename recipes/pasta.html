Pasta Bake with Cherry Tomatoes and Basil

My whole family loves it, and there is not much prep. Once the pasta bake is in the oven, you can make a salad or set the table, and then it's time to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 (6 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 ¼ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 small bunch fresh basil, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a baking dish.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes. Drain, reserving 1 cup cooking water.
  3. A large colander with cooked penne pasta, with a small measuring cup of pasta water
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook onion in oil until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  5. A large pan with chopped onion and garlic being stirred with a wooden spoon
  6. Stir in tomato sauce and tomato paste and cook until slightly reduced, about 5 minutes.
  7. A pan with tomato sauce and tomato paste, with chopped onions and garlic
  8. Add cream and Parmesan cheese. Season with sugar, salt, and pepper.
  9. A pan of creamy tomato sauce with salt, pepper, and sugar being stirred in with a wooden spoon
  10. Stir some of the reserved pasta water into sauce and add cooked penne.
  11. A pan of penne pasta in creamy tomato sauce with cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil
  12. Pour penne mixture into the prepared baking dish and cover with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  13. A baking dish with creamy tomato penne pasta, topped with shredded mozzarella cheese
  14. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and cheese is melted, about 20 minutes.
